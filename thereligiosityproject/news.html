Why does this matter to me?

Religious news is some of the most unloved, underreported, or underrated content out there, and that's a problem. When religion does make the headlines it's almost always tied to conflict, controversy, or politics. What you rarely see is the full picture. Faith communities around the world are doing genuinely remarkable things every single day and most of it never makes the news cycle. We think that needs to change.

How we help.

Our news page exists to cover religion the way it actually deserves to be covered, with the same seriousness and depth you'd want for any other major force in human life. That means covering the hard stories when they come up, but it also means making sure the good ones get just as much attention. Religion has always been one of the most powerful drivers of charity, community, and positive change in the world, and we're here to make sure that story gets told too.
